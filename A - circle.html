Ctrl + Meyer is a team of Grade 12 Senior High School students from Pasig City Science High School of nearby areas in Pasig City, united by creativity, curiosity, and a drive to explore technology and design. Each member brings unique interests and skills from animation, content creation, and layout design to idea generation and problem-solving.

So, what more about them?

Their mission is to promote creativity, create engaging learning experiences, inspire others, and enhance their skills when using technology.

This group values teamwork, communication, and creativity, allowing one to use their full potential in creating.

Through effective communication and mutual support, the group aims to produce high-quality outputs that demonstrate their passion and effort in creating useful innovations.
